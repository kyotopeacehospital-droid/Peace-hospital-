Why "Peace"

The hospital's — 平和 (heiwa), peace — reflects : patients heal faster in an environment built around calm, not just clinical efficiency. Every department is designed to reduce the friction and anxiety that usually come with hospital visits, from a records system that respects privacy, to a physical space that feels more like a retreat than a waiting room.
